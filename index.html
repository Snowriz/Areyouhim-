Friendship

Friendship is a bond between two or more people who share mutual affection, trust, and support. It is based on shared experiences, communication, and understanding. Friends are there to provide emotional support during tough times, celebrate good moments, and offer advice. A true friend is someone who respects, listens, and helps when needed.

In many cultures, friendship is considered one of the most valuable relationships in life, offering a sense of belonging and companionship. No matter the distance, real friends remain close at heart.
